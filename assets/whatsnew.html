Release 1.2

  • A to B to C routing ...
    Need to pop to the shops on the way to work? Want to map out a route taking in your favourite shops? Now you can, because routes can now include intermediate waypoints. Simply tap on the map to set start, tap again to set the first waypoint, tap again for the second, and so on, up to a maximum of twelve. Hit the "Tap here to Route" banner to find your route.
  • Location button shows whether you are following or just showing your current location.
  • Changes to Add Photo to prevent duplicate uploads.
  • Routing finding should now work on all 3G networks.

Release 1.1

  • New Points of Interest main menu option. Show points of interest, including cycle parking, pubs, cafes, post boxes, and London Cycle Hire points, on the route map. Choose the icon size from the settings menu.
  • New main menu option Open route number. Want to load a journey you planned on the website? Just type in the journey number and off you go. Journey number is also shown on the itinerary tab.
  • Saved routes can be renamed. Press and hold on a saved route to bring up the menu, then choose Rename.
  • Itinerary now includes the estimated CO2 saving from cycling rather than driving, along with the calories used on the journey.
  • Photomap categories are preloaded and cached.
